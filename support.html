How to Use Chess Journal

Chess Journal is designed to help you track, review, and share your chess games effortlessly. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Onboarding: When you open the app for the first time, complete the onboarding process to set your player name and preferences.
  • Recording a Game: Navigate to the "Your Games" tab and click the "+" button to add a new game. Fill in the game details and save.
  • Analyzing Moves: Select a game to view its board and move history. You can replay moves, export the game as a PGN file, or manually set the game state.
  • Customizing Appearance: Visit the "Preferences" tab to change themes, enable coordinates, or update your player name.
