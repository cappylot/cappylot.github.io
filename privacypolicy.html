Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20.12.2024

Last Updated: 20.12.2024

At Chess Journal, your privacy is of utmost importance. I want to emphasize that no data is collected, shared, or transmitted externally. All information is stored securely on your device.

What Data Is Stored Locally?

All data related to your usage of Chess Journal is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Player Information: If you provide a player name, it is stored locally for use in game records and exports.
  • Game Data: Your saved games, moves, and states, as well as any Portable Game Notation (PGN) files you export.
  • Preferences and Settings: Themes, appearance preferences, and other app settings are stored locally.

No Data Collection or Sharing

Chess Journal does not collect, store, or share any data externally. All your data stays on your device, ensuring complete privacy. I do not use external servers, analytics tools, or third-party services.

Data Deletion

If you uninstall Chess Journal, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted from your device. You can manage your data directly through the app, including editing or deleting game records and preferences.
